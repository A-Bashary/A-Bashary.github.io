Privacy Policy

At Double Double Pizza and Chicken, we believe the privacy of our visitors is an essential part of building a long-term trusting relationship. To that end, we've developed a Privacy Statement that exceeds those normally seen on the Internet and meets upcoming Canadian legislation (Bill C-6). These guidelines have been developed with the recognition that Internet technologies are rapidly evolving, and that underlying business models are still not established. Accordingly, guidelines are subject to change. Any such changes will be posted on this page. Double Double Pizza and Chicken is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of Web sites to which it may link or sites that link to it.

Accountability

We take your privacy seriously. Double Double Pizza and Chicken has specific individuals ensuring that we're in compliance with our Privacy Statement and any Canadian legislation. We also train our employees regularly on ethical privacy policies and practices.

Identifying Purposes

Double Double Pizza and Chicken requires your name and your city when contacting us through our "contact us" web form; however, we do not provide this information to third parties.

Consent

All of our marketing initiatives for individuals who have given us their contact information are permission based. If you don't want to be contacted by us, you won't be.

Limiting Collection, Individual Access & Our Openness

Double Double Pizza & Chicken collects technical information like IP addresses in order to maximize efficiency and to analyze system performance. This data is not shared, and it is stored in a secure manner.

Limiting Use, Disclosure & Retention

Double Double Pizza and Chicken may share the information it gathers, in aggregate, with third parties. We do not release personal information about you as an individual to third parties and we encourage you to only provide information that you're comfortable with. If you would like your user name and other personal information erased from our records, we'll process your request without hesitation.

Data Safeguards

Your data is kept encrypted within the most secure database platform available.
